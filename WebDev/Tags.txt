Headings (block : meaning they take more than a line you can press enter and continue)
          <h1>, <h2>,....,<h6>...etc

Paragraphs (block)
          <p>......</p>
          should only contain inline elements (same line tags)

Divs (block)
        <div>.....</div>
        Generic section that is larger than a paragraph

Ordered list
<ol> (ordered list)
    <li> Item One </li> (list)
    <li> Item Two </li>
</ol>    

Line breaks
<br>

Unordered list
<ul>
    <li> Item One </li>
    <li> Item Two </li>
</ul>
